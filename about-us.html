About Us


Association of Computing Machinary ACM, Chitkara University. This is the first Chapter of ACM at Chitkara university. It is under the Computer Science department of Chitkara University.

In ACM Chapter, we aim to explore new technologies and Research new Facts about the upcoming and existing technologies. Here, Chapter will organize dynamic activity hubs so the enthusiast meet, interacts and learn from each other. The chapter also going to organize various events which include hackathons, technical workshops, some research talks, etc.
